### 1. Disease Diagnosis and Detection
AI algorithms are used to detect diseases such as cancer, diabetes, heart disease, and neurological disorders by analyzing medical images, lab reports, and patient records.

### 2. Medical Imaging
AI helps in analyzing X-rays, MRIs, CT scans, and ultrasounds with high precision, reducing human error and improving early detection of diseases.

### 3. Personalized Treatment
AI enables personalized medicine by analyzing patient history, genetics, and lifestyle to recommend customized treatment plans.

### 4. Drug Discovery and Development
AI accelerates drug discovery by predicting molecular behavior, reducing research time, and lowering development costs.

### 5. Virtual Health Assistants
AI-powered chatbots and virtual assistants help patients with appointment scheduling, medication reminders, and basic medical advice.

### 6. Remote Patient Monitoring
AI-based wearable devices monitor patient health in real time, helping doctors track vital signs and manage chronic diseases remotely.

### 7. Robotic Surgery
AI-assisted robots support surgeons by providing precision, flexibility, and control during complex surgical procedures.
